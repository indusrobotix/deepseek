Our Story & Mission

Building Pakistan's robotics future, one kit at a time

From Passion to Profession

IndusRoboTix was founded in 2026 by Furqan Khatti with a simple mission: to make quality robotics accessible and affordable in Pakistan. What started as a personal passion for robotics evolved into a business dedicated to supporting STEM education and innovation across the country.

Frustrated by the high costs and limited availability of quality robotics components in Pakistan, Furqan began sourcing components locally and assembling custom kits for fellow enthusiasts. The unique value proposition of custom-designed acrylic chassis quickly set IndusRoboTix apart from imported alternatives.

Today, we proudly serve students, hobbyists, educational institutions, and professionals throughout Pakistan, providing locally sourced, high-quality robotics solutions that empower innovation and learning.

Our Mission & Values

Local Empowerment

We believe in supporting Pakistan's economy by sourcing components locally and contributing to the growth of the local tech ecosystem.

Education First

Our primary goal is to make robotics education accessible to all Pakistanis, regardless of background or budget constraints.

Innovation Through Customization

We empower creativity by providing customizable solutions that allow users to bring their unique robotics ideas to life.

Quality & Reliability

Every component is tested, and every kit is assembled with care to ensure our customers receive reliable, durable products.
