The Journey Begins

With a background in electrical engineering and a passion for robotics that began in university, Furqan founded IndusRoboTix in 2026 to address the gap in affordable, quality robotics solutions in Pakistan.

His journey started as a university student frustrated by the lack of accessible robotics components in the local market. What began as a personal project to source components for his own robotics experiments quickly evolved into a mission to make robotics accessible to all Pakistanis.

Today, Furqan leads a team of engineers and educators dedicated to advancing STEM education through practical, hands-on robotics learning experiences.

Core Motivation

"I believe every Pakistani student should have access to quality robotics education, regardless of their background or budget constraints. That's why we're building IndusRoboTix - to empower the next generation of innovators."
