Our Story & Mission

Building Pakistan's robotics future, one kit at a time

"Our mission is to democratize robotics education in Pakistan."

FOUNDER & CEO

Furqan Khatti

Electrical Engineer & Robotics Enthusiast

With a background in electrical engineering and a passion for robotics that began in university, Furqan founded IndusRoboTix in 2026 to address the gap in affordable, quality robotics solutions in Pakistan.

His journey started as a university student frustrated by the lack of accessible robotics components in the local market. What began as a personal project to source components for his own robotics experiments quickly evolved into a mission to make robotics accessible to all Pakistanis.

Today, Furqan leads a team of engineers and educators dedicated to advancing STEM education through practical, hands-on robotics learning experiences.

From Passion to Profession

2026

The Beginning

IndusRoboTix is founded with a simple mission: to make quality robotics accessible and affordable in Pakistan. Starting from a small workshop, Furqan begins assembling basic kits for fellow university students.

2027

First Breakthrough

We partner with our first educational institution, marking our entry into formal STEM education. The custom acrylic chassis design becomes our signature feature, setting us apart from imported alternatives.

2028

Expanding Reach

Launch of our advanced robotics kits and expansion of services across major Pakistani cities. We begin conducting workshops and training programs for schools and universities.

Present

Pakistan's Robotics Partner

Serving hundreds of educational institutions, hobbyists, and professionals across Pakistan. Continuously innovating with new kit designs and educational resources.
