Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How long does custom assembly take?

Standard assembly takes 3-5 business days. Complex custom projects may take 7-10 days. We'll provide a specific timeline during consultation.

Can I provide my own components for assembly?

Yes, we can assemble kits using components you provide. We'll test everything to ensure compatibility and functionality.

Do you offer warranty on your services?

Yes! All our services come with a 3-month warranty. Custom chassis designs have a 6-month warranty against manufacturing defects.

What areas do you serve for workshops?

We conduct workshops across Pakistan, with regular sessions in major cities. Contact us to schedule a workshop in your location.

Can I visit your workshop?

Yes! We welcome visitors by appointment. Contact us to schedule a visit to see our assembly process and facilities.

Do you offer after-sales support?

Absolutely! We provide free technical support for 6 months after purchase. Extended support plans are also available.
