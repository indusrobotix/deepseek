About IndusRoboTix

Founded in 2026 by Furqan Khatti, IndusRoboTix is a Pakistani-based business specializing in creating custom robotic kits from locally sourced components.

We combine high-quality robotic parts with custom-designed acrylic chassis to create unique robotics kits suitable for enthusiasts, students, and professionals.

Our mission is to make robotics accessible and affordable in Pakistan while promoting STEM education and innovation in the field of robotics and automation.
